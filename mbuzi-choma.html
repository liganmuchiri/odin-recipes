Mbuzi choma recipe


Description

This is a well described mbuzi choma recipe
It outspans a cultural method of cooking mbuzi meat especially on family gatherings or celebrations
Its blended with various ingredients and served with its favourite accompaniment ugali or kachumbari.
Its well enjoyed communally in outdoor shared platters

Ingredients

  • ribs, front and back section of a mbuzi
  • 5 onions
  • 5 tomatoes
  • 1 ginger
  • 1 tumeric
  • salt
  • 1 pilipili

Steps

  1. put the meat section in a big sufuria and add water to the level of the meat
  2. Heat vigorously until it boils
  3. grit ginger and tumeric and add to the boiling sufuria
  4. after the meat is tender separate it from the sufuria and put it in a gaze under burning charcoal
  5. keep turning the meat adding salt until a refined color is achieved
  6. chop the tomatoes, onions and the pilipili and mix it well in a dish to have the kachumbari
  7. serve the meat alongside the kachumari in a big platter while still hot
