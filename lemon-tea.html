Description

This is a fabuluous script on how to prepare lemon tea to give it its luring smell and amazing taste
lemon tea is best when served hot to meet its taste
It blends well in the morning to give you that steam and energy all day long

Ingredients

  • 1 fresh lemon
  • 2 tablespoonful of sugar
  • 1 sachet of cofee

Steps

  1. put 1 cup of water in a sufuria and heat if over a flame
  2. cut the lemon into two and put it in the sufuria
  3. add the coffee and the sugar
  4. allow it to boil for 15 to a brown gold color
  5. remove the boiled emon pieces and use a lemon squeezer to extract juice back to the sufuria
  6. sieve the rest into a cup and enjoy
