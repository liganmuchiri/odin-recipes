Description

Pilau is one of the most widely used meals, mainly due to its taste
Below we outline the best procedure to ensure the meal achieves its best flavour
It is well served with chapati alongside stew or fried meat

Ingredients

  • 1 kg pishori rice
  • 1 kg meat
  • 1/2 litre c/oil
  • 2 large onions
  • 1 cinamon stick
  • 1 ginger
  • 1 garlic
  • 1 tumeric
  • pilau masala
  • salt

Steps

  1. add the c/oil in a sufuria and heat over a flame
  2. chop the onions, garlic and tumeric and add to the sufuria
  3. stir gently to achieve a brown color
  4. add meat and continue stiring for about 10 minutes
  5. add pilau masala and stir to mix well
  6. add water to just cover the meat
  7. cover the latter allowing it to boil until the meat is tender
  8. add rice and cinnamon
  9. add hot water to cover the rice and heat to boil
  10. stir gently to allow the ingredients mix well
  11. reduce heat and add salt to taste
  12. serve with minji stew and chapati
